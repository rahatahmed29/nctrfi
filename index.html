National Conference on Transformative Research and Its Future Impacts (NCTRFI)

We are pleased to invite you to the National Conference on Transformative Research and Its Future Impacts (NCTRFI), to be held on 25 June 2025 at Khulna University, Khulna-9208. The Research and Innovation Centre will organize it. This national-level conference brings together leading minds from academia, research, and industry to explore pioneering ideas and impactful innovations in the fields of technology, education, sustainability, and future-oriented research. The conference will provide a vibrant platform for students, researchers, and academics to exchange insights, present original work, and engage in thoughtful dialogue on how transformative research can shape a better, more sustainable future. We warmly invite you to participate by submitting your research paper, attending the technical sessions, or contributing as a poster presenter. Your presence and expertise would greatly enrich the discourse and collaboration this event aims to foster.

Please send your abstract and/or poster to office@ric.ku.ac.bd or director@ric.ku.ac.bd, and visit our website (https://ric.ku.ac.bd/) for detailed information regarding registration, paper submission, and important deadlines. We look forward to your valuable participation in making NCTRFI a landmark event in advancing transformative research and innovation.
